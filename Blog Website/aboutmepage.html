About Me

Who is Abhinav?

Abhinav Ayyau Kamalakannan is a sophomore at Mountain House High School with a passion for computer hardware, stock market analysis, basketball, and travel. He was born in Chennai, India, and his family moved to New Jersey of the United States at a young age. Abhinav's curiosity and drive to learn something new every day took him places he never imagined, and he still continues to do just that.

My Journey

Early Interests

Growing up, I always tended to ask people around me how things worked. For example, I would ask my parents about ceiling fans, and how one might spin slow, medium, or fast. I also was interested in understanding how phones worked, or even how cars functioned. As a side note, I was also a very invested Hotwheels collector as a kid, and I would often pick one up everytime I went to the store. Which led me to my next point of interest - cars! I was fascinated by cars, and would often watch videos about them on YouTube, Netflix, and other platforms. My favorite show is on Prime VIdeow, called "The Grand Tour", which features Jeremy Clarkson, Richard Hammond, and James May traveling the world and testing out cars in various areas. As I moved to California in 2021, I happened to friends with a car enthusiast who increased my interest in cars even more.

Recent Interests

I started playing Basketball when I was 14, and I instantly fell in love with the sport. The culture, the overall competitiveness, and the overall IQ required to play the game intrigued me. I currently play in AAU, and have still yet to make my high school team, but I am determined to keep improving. On the academic side of things, I have recently developed an interest in the stock market, thanks to my Dad. My Dad opened up my first stock account several months ago and I have been learning about stocks, investing, and trading ever since. I also developed an interest in computer hardware from watching various influencers on YouTube, and the overall idea of a custom built PC fascinated me. I then decided to build my own PC in mid-2025, and I have been interested about computer hardware components, and PCs ever since. I normally read articles, and watch videos about these topics on a daily basis learning more information along the way.
